House Old-Fashioned

Rye, demerara, Angostura, orange peel.

§ 01Ingredients

  • 2 ozRittenhouse rye
  • ¼ ozdemerara syrup (2:1)
  • 2 dashesAngostura bitters
  • 1 dashorange bitters
  • 1large rock
  • 1orange peel, expressed

§ 02Ratios

spirit : sweet : bitter
8 : 1 : 0.05

§ 03Method

  1. Step 01Combine rye, demerara, both bitters in a mixing glass with cracked ice.
  2. Step 02Stir 25 – 30 turns, until the glass beads and the liquid is silky.
  3. Step 03Strain over a large rock in a chilled rocks glass.
  4. Step 04Express orange peel over the surface; drape over the rim.
  5. Step 05Drink slowly. The whole point is to watch it open up.
Iteration notes.

v3 · Switched from beef chuck to bone-in short rib. Decisively better.

v2 · Added coffee for depth; didn't taste like coffee at the end. Stayed.

v1 · Original. Salt low. Cook time short. Don't go back.
