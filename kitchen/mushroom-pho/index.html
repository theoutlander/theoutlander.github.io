Mushroom Pho

Charred onion, dried shiitake, star anise. Vegetarian.

§ 01Ingredients

  • 1.2 kgbone-in short ribs
  • 1yellow onion, diced
  • 3garlic cloves, smashed
  • 1 Tbsptomato paste
  • 20 gdark cocoa (Valrhona)
  • 200 mlstrong brewed coffee
  • 500 mldry red wine
  • 500 mlbeef stock
  • —kosher salt · pepper · bay

§ 02Ratios

meat : liquid
1 : 1
oven
150 °C · 3 h 30 m

§ 03Method

  1. Step 01Salt the short ribs generously the night before. Refrigerate uncovered.
  2. Step 02Sear in a heavy Dutch oven over medium-high. All sides; don't rush.
  3. Step 03Reduce heat. Soften onion and garlic in the rendered fat. Add tomato paste; cook until brick-red.
  4. Step 04Add cocoa, then deglaze with coffee and wine; reduce by a third.
  5. Step 05Return ribs; add stock to barely cover. Lid on. 150 °C for 3 h 30 m.
  6. Step 06Rest 20 minutes. The braise should slide; the meat should yield to a spoon.
  7. Step 07Skim, reduce sauce on the stove if needed. Serve with polenta.
Iteration notes.

v3 · Switched from beef chuck to bone-in short rib. Decisively better.

v2 · Added coffee for depth; didn't taste like coffee at the end. Stayed.

v1 · Original. Salt low. Cook time short. Don't go back.
